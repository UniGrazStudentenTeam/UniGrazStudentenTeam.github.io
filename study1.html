What was done?

The authors created and validated an anxiety level prediction technology, based on the data from a wristband (blood volume pressure, galvanic skin response, body temperature) meant to be used in VR Exposure therapy. They trained and evaluated their model on 30 participants undergoing a Virtual Reality Exposure treatment for public speaking.
